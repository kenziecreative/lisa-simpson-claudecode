## Engagement Strategy

**Respond to comments within 2 hours** with:
- Answers to technical questions
- Links to relevant resources (docs, case studies, demos)
- Personal invitations to schedule demos for serious prospects

**Monitor mentions** of:
- TechFlow, #EnterpriseAnalytics, #BusinessIntelligence
- Competitor names (Tableau, Power BI, Looker)
- Common pain points ("BI is too slow", "need self-service analytics")

**Amplification**:
- Tag customer companies (with permission) in customer proof posts
- Employee advocacy: Share posts with team for likes/reshares
- Engage with relevant industry conversations to build visibility

## Performance Metrics

**Track**:
- Impressions, reach, engagement rate per post
- Click-through rate to landing page
- Demo requests attributed to social
- Follower growth during campaign
- Share of voice vs. competitors

**Optimize**:
- Best performing post formats (carousel vs. text vs. video)
- Optimal posting times by platform
- Topics with highest engagement (technical, use case, customer proof)
- CTAs that drive most conversions
